Fshiki j.P Setup

After certain H-Kabari knockdowns, Baiken can setup a fshiki.

What is an fshiki?
A fshiki, or fuzzy overhead, is an instant overhead that hits rising even when they block low.
Normally when you press a Button while rising, your button will whiff as the opponent "crouches" under your attack. With a Fuzzy overhead however, your button will still hit. That is because they are stuck in a standing animation after standing up and even if they block low, their hurtbox is extended upwards for a few frames.
Because of that, you can hit a j.P while rising.

Right here however, our j.H is the thing that is enabling us to hit a fshiki.
If we hit j.H perfectly meaty it's +10 on block. Since they have to standblock to not get hit, we can now fshiki/fuzzy overhead with j.P

To get the perfect meaty, simply framekill after H-Kabari~H with 5P.
Then you have to jump with 8 (Up) and Instan-Airdash j.H.
Pretty nice and easy setup :3
