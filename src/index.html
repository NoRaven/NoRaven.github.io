This website serves as an helpful guide for either new Baiken players, or for Baiken veterans that want to up their game.
Baiken has, even when she is kind of an unga bunga character, technical demands for her combos and setups. That's why a lot of the setups and inputs are all written out very extensively. So that even a complete beginner to fighting games can understand the stuff I'm talking about.

This website has been written by NoRaven.
